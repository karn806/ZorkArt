Goal of this game: Gain EXP to the requirement to win! Yay!!

Commands:
1. info {player,room}
2. take {item}
3. use {potion,item}
4. go {direction}
    1. north
    2. south
    3. west
    4. east
5. attack {monster}
7. help
8. quit

Items:
1. Revive potion:
    1. Normal potion (+10HP)
    2. Golden Potion (+20HP)
    3. Platinum Potion (MAX HP)

There are 2 ranks of monsters.
1. 'Monmon' - monster rank 1 (10HP)
3. 'King Mon' - monster rank 2 (20HP)

Player's Properties:
1. HP - Health Point
2. EXP - Experience Point
3. ATK - Attack Point

Constraints
A player initially fight with bare hands (so strong)
A player have maximum Health Point (HP) of 100 points, but initially given 30 HP.
A monster is killed, you gain Experience by 2
